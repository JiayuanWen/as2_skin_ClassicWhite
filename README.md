## Download & Install

### Steam Workshop
If you already own Audiosurf 2 on Steam, you can subscribe to this skin [here](https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1157230034).

### Manual install
If you own Audiosurf 2 from other sources or Steam Workshop doesn't work for you, you can download the skin archive [here](https://github.com/JiayuanWen/as2_skin_ClassicWhite/releases). Extract the skin folder inside to your game's skin directory:
  * On Windows: `C:\Program Files (x86)\Steam\steamapps\common\Audiosurf 2\skins`
  * On Linux & Steam Deck: `~/.steam/steam/steamapps/common/Audiosurf 2/skins`
  * MacOS: `~/Library/Application Support/Steam/steamapps/common/Audiosurf 2/skins`

    <sup>Note: Your path might differ if you configured Steam to store your games somewhere else.</sup>
<br>
<br>

## Configurations
This skin provide several options you can tweak to suit your personal taste.

To tweak the skin, find and open the skin's lua file:
* On Windows: `C:\Program Files (x86)\Steam\steamapps\common\Audiosurf 2\skins\76561198099088813\classic (white)\classic (white).lua`
* On Linux: `~/.steam/steam/steamapps/common/Audiosurf 2/skins/76561198099088813/classic (white)/classic (white).lua`
* On MacOS: `~/Library/Application Support/Steam/steamapps/common/Audiosurf 2/skins/76561198099088813/classic (white)/classic (white).lua`

    <sup>Note: Your path might differ if you configured Steam to store your games somewhere else.</sup>
<br>

At the top of the file you'll see the following options:

### Competitive camera
This skin has two camera modes: **Normal camera** and **Competitive camera**. The Normal camera is the intended camera for the skin, recreating the camera movement of the original. Competitive camera raise the camera for better visibility, aimed at making competitive play easier.

Option name: `competitiveCamera`
<br>

### Spectrum/Mono road color
You can toggle whether the road be rendered the same color throughout or possess different color at different sections depending on the song's intensity.

Option name: `oneColorCliff`
<br>

### Road length toggle
You can toggle whether to show road entirely or partial.

Option name: `showEntireRoad`
<br>

### Graphical elements toggle
You can toggle visibility of rings, background buildings, and skywire. 

Option names:
* `showRing` for rings along the track
* `showBackgroundBuilding` for background objects 
* `showSkyWire` for wires in the sky
* `showAirBubbles` for grey bubbles floating around the track
